Numbers

The number symbols are images of counting on the right hand. The corner represents the thumb, and on 4 you flip over to the back of the hand.
You can see that this order is correct by looking at the top of hub texts and see that they are increasing, and wall texts and see that they form all the numbers between 1 and 67
