Hub Walls Texts

The game's entry zone (nicknamed "the hub") contains a series of wall panels between the relic room and Bodach's statue. Here you can find the meaning of these panels and learn the lore behind Phantom Abyss.
For some panels, we aren't sure what the meaning is yet and they are marked as Tentative or Not yet translated.

If you would like to help translating the texts or think we have something wrong (we probably do!), please come join the conversation on the discord in the #ancient-language channel! We’d love to hear your voice
